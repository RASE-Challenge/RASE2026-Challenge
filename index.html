## About The **RASE 2026 Challenge** at **ICASSP 2026** focuses on speech enhancement from **mmWave radar** data through glass barriers. Participants will receive radar-microphone paired data, develop enhancement models, and compete for top rankings.
---
## Dataset Radar signals are recorded using the TI AWR2243 mmWave radar. The challenge includes 3 difficulty levels: - Loudspeaker diaphragm (simple) - Secondary surface vibrations - Laptop screen in a virtual meeting setup (hard) Paired microphone recordings are provided in `.wav` format.
---
## Evaluation Metrics used: - **PESQ** - **ESTOI** - **DNSMOS** - **MFCC Cosine Similarity** Scores are weighted based on difficulty.
---
